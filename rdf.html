We have chosen to represent in RDF the item that more than the others was able to connect people, places, dates and concepts, that is the Sketch of the Analytical Engine, the book written by Menabrea on Babbage's Analytical Engine and translated by Ada Lovelace.

Turtle was chosen as a serialization of RDF.
